OwnaFleet · Partner overview

For advisors with a book of high-income clients.

A managed equipment ownership program built for the kind of client you already work with — high-earning professionals and windfall recipients who need a real, tax-advantaged asset. You refer; we run the rest.

The 30-second version

Your client takes direct title to ~$1M of heavy equipment, financed roughly 90/10. The equipment immediately enters a managed rental fleet operated by a major U.S. publicly-traded rental company. They get monthly distributions, a Year-1 tax overlay via 100% bonus depreciation (restored under the One Big Beautiful Bill Act for property placed in service after Jan 19, 2025), and a real cash-flowing asset on their balance sheet — not a security, not a fund, not a paper product.

You stay in the trusted-advisor seat. We run the conversation, the credit application, the closing, and the lifetime support. You get 0.84% of the equipment purchase price on every closed deal — about $8,400 on a $1M deal, and your fee scales with the deal size.

Why your clients fit

The program is designed for two situations advisors see constantly:

Door 1

Diversifying a windfall

Practice sale, business exit, stock sale, inheritance, or other liquidity event. Client has cash, doesn't want all of it in public markets, and is looking for something real that produces income — not just paper.

~80% of program participants come through this door.

Door 2

Offsetting a strong-income year

High-income W-2 / K-1 year — bonus, promotion, partnership distribution, IPO vest — where the client needs ordinary-income shelter and 100% bonus depreciation is the leverage point. Equipment doesn't have the cap-rate compression of real estate.

Specifically suited for:

  • Dentists, orthodontists, and specialists — especially those mid-career, post-practice-sale, or in a heavy-income year
  • Physicians and surgeons with $500K+ HHI and limited tax shelter options
  • Business owners selling LLC interests or stock with substantial capital gains
  • Senior executives with concentrated equity comp events
  • Active real-estate sponsors who want non-correlated, lower-management-burden tax-advantaged income

Qualification floor

  • $1M+ net worth (lender requires 3× equipment value)
  • $300K+ liquidity (covers down payment + reserves)
  • Personal guarantee acceptable — the financing is recourse
