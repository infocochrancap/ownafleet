How it works

Three returns. Five steps.

Heavy equipment generates rental income, qualifies for an accelerated-depreciation election, and holds residual value as a real, durable asset. The program lets you participate without operating a rental business yourself.

Three returns from one asset
01
Monthly distributions
Equipment generates rental revenue from day one. Owners receive their share of net rental revenue, distributed monthly to their LLC's operating account.
02
Accelerated depreciation
Heavy equipment qualifies for the available accelerated-depreciation election under current tax law, potentially offsetting other income in Year 1. For most participants, the after-tax economics are the headline. Your CPA confirms the specifics.

Real, durable asset
Mobile, dollar-priced industrial equipment serving long-cycle infrastructure spend. The operating partner provides a meaningful downside floor on residual value at end of term.

Who This Is For

An honest gut check.

This program isn't right for everyone. Lenders require specific financial qualifications, and the asset class needs the right kind of holder. Here's the straight answer on fit.

You're likely a fit if…

  • Net worth ≥ 3× equipment purchase ($3M+ for a $1M deal)
  • Liquid assets ≥ 30% of purchase ($300K+ for a $1M deal)
  • Comfortable with personal guarantee on the financed portion
  • Looking to offset W-2, K-1, or business income with depreciation
  • Want exposure to infrastructure & industrial spend without operating a business

It may not be right if…

  • You can't tolerate cash flow variability month-to-month
  • You need full liquidity in less than 6 years
  • You don't have ordinary income to absorb the depreciation
  • You're uncomfortable with a long-term operational dependency on a single counterparty
  • You'd require investor-style governance rights (this is an asset purchase, not a fund)
