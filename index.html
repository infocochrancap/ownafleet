What it is

A different kind of real asset.

A turnkey program for individuals to own specific heavy equipment that's rented out across the U.S. by a major publicly-traded operating partner. You hold direct title through your own LLC. The operator runs the rental business. The entire structure is built so you don't have to.

01
You own it directly
Specific, serialized equipment titled to your LLC. Not a fund interest, not pooled, not a security — a real asset you can identify, track, and sell.
02
They run it
A national, publicly-traded rental operator manages enrollment, customers, maintenance, telematics, and remarketing. You don't operate a business — you make a capital decision and collect.
03
Tax-advantaged by structure
The federal tax code rewards real-asset ownership with accelerated-depreciation elections. Whether and how those apply depends on your situation — your CPA confirms before you sign.
Who it's for

Is this for you?

Most participants come through one of two doors. The program is built for both, but the specifics of fit are best worked out on a call.

Diversifying a windfall

  • A recent capital gain, business sale, or strong investment year
  • Looking for something real — uncorrelated to public markets
  • Want cash flow without operating a business yourself
  • Comfortable holding through the program's contract horizon

Offsetting a high-income year

  • Active business, equity comp, or K-1 generating meaningful taxable income
  • Your CPA has flagged accelerated-depreciation strategies as relevant
  • Open to a multi-year horizon, not a one-off transaction
  • Comfortable with a personal guarantee on financed equipment
This program likely isn't a fit if: you need full liquidity inside a 6-year window · you don't have an income or tax profile that benefits from accelerated depreciation · you'd want investor-style governance rights (this is direct ownership, not a fund interest) · you'd prefer a pooled-vehicle or fund-style structure.
